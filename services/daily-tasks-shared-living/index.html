Service

Daily Tasks / Shared Living

Support with routine activities and shared living tasks to build stability and confidence.

About this support

This service helps participants manage everyday responsibilities, develop practical life routines, and maintain a supportive and stable living environment.

This support is delivered with a person-centred approach and can be adapted to each participant's goals, routines, and preferences.

Make a Referral Enquire

What this service includes

  • Assistance with daily routines and practical tasks
  • Support within shared living arrangements
  • Routine planning based on participant goals
  • Skill development through consistent guidance
  • Flexible support for changing day-to-day needs

Expected outcomes

  • More independent daily living
  • Better routine consistency and structure
  • Improved confidence in managing shared environments

Need additional support areas combined? Our team can help tailor a plan across multiple services.
