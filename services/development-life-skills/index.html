Service

Development-Life Skills

Skill-building supports focused on greater independence and everyday capability.

About this support

This service helps participants build practical life skills across home, social, and community contexts to support long-term independence and confidence.

This support is delivered with a person-centred approach and can be adapted to each participant's goals, routines, and preferences.

Make a Referral Enquire

What this service includes

  • Coaching for everyday living and personal organisation
  • Support to build communication and social skills
  • Practical routines for independence at home
  • Goal tracking to reinforce progress over time
  • Participant-centred support pacing and structure

Expected outcomes

  • Improved everyday capability and confidence
  • Greater independence in practical life tasks
  • Steady progress toward personal development goals

Need additional support areas combined? Our team can help tailor a plan across multiple services.
